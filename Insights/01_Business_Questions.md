# 📊 Business Questions – Instagram Influencer Analysis

## 🎯 Core Business Objective
To identify high-performing influencers for brand collaborations using data-driven evaluation.

---

## 🧠 Key Business Questions

### 1. Influencer Performance
- Who are the top-performing influencers based on reach and engagement?
- Which influencers provide the best engagement efficiency per follower?

---

### 2. Audience Analysis
- How is follower distribution spread across influencers?
- Are high-follower influencers actually generating high engagement?

---

### 3. Content Strategy
- Does posting frequency impact engagement rate?
- Which influencers maintain consistent content output?

---

### 4. Market Segmentation
- Which countries have the highest number of influencers?
- How does influencer performance vary by geography?

---

### 5. Brand Collaboration Strategy
- Which influencers are best suited for micro vs macro campaigns?
- Which influencers provide the best ROI potential for brands?

---

### 6. Data Quality & Reliability
- Are there missing or unknown country values affecting analysis?
- Is engagement data consistent across all influencers?
