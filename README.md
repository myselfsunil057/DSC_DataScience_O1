# 📊 Instagram Influencer Analysis – Data-Driven Brand Ambassador Selection

## 🧠 Project Overview
This project analyzes Instagram influencer data to identify the most valuable brand ambassadors using data analytics techniques.  
The goal is to move from manual influencer selection to a **data-driven decision system** based on reach, engagement, activity, and influence metrics.

Instead of relying on subjective judgment, this system evaluates influencers using measurable KPIs and ranking logic.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To help brands identify **high-performing micro and macro influencers** by analyzing:

- Audience reach
- Engagement quality
- Content activity
- Influence strength
- Geographic distribution

---

## 📁 Dataset Information
The dataset contains Instagram influencer-level performance metrics:

### 📌 Columns:
- `rank` – Influencer ranking position  
- `channel_info` – Influencer username  
- `influence_score` – Overall influence rating  
- `posts` – Total number of posts  
- `followers` – Total followers  
- `avg_likes` – Average likes per post  
- `60_day_eng_rate` – Engagement rate over last 60 days  
- `new_post_avg_like` – Average likes on new posts  
- `total_likes` – Total likes received  
- `country` – Influencer location  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning Process
Key transformations performed:

- Converted followers (M/K/B) into numeric format
- Standardized likes and post counts
- Converted engagement rate (%) into decimal format
- Handled missing country values (marked as "Unknown")
- Ensured consistent data types for analysis

---

## 📊 KPIs Designed
The dashboard includes the following business KPIs:

- 👥 **Influencer Count** – Total number of influencers analyzed  
- 🌍 **Total Audience Reach** – Sum of all followers  
- 📈 **Average Engagement Rate** – Interaction efficiency across influencers  
- ❤️ **Average Post Engagement** – Mean likes per post  
- 🏆 **Average Influence Score** – Overall credibility index  
- ⚡ **Engagement Efficiency** – Likes per follower ratio  
- 🧾 **Total Content Output** – Total posts across influencers  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 👥 Audience Scale
- Mega influencers dominate reach distribution
- Top accounts control majority of total audience exposure

### 📈 Engagement Performance
- Engagement rates vary significantly across influencers
- Mid-tier influencers often outperform mega accounts in interaction rate

### 🧾 Content Activity
- High posting frequency does not guarantee high engagement
- Quality > quantity in content strategy

### 🌍 Geographic Distribution
- United States dominates influencer presence
- Brazil is the second-largest contributor
- Significant portion of data has unknown country values

---

## 🏆 Top Influencers (Example Insight)
Top influencers by reach include:

- Cristiano Ronaldo  
- Kylie Jenner  
- Lionel Messi  
- Selena Gomez  
- Dwayne Johnson  

These accounts dominate **audience scale**, but not always engagement efficiency.

---

## ⚠️ Key Business Insights
- High followers ≠ high engagement  
- Engagement efficiency is a better KPI than raw reach  
- Influencer selection should balance:
  - Reach
  - Engagement
  - Activity
  - Influence score
